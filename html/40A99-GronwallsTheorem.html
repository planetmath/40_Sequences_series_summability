Gronwall’s theorem


The function

G(n):=σ⁢(n)n⁢ln⁡(ln⁡n)  (n= 2, 3, 4,…),

in which σ⁢(n) means the sum of the positive divisors of n, satisfies the equation

lim supn→∞⁡G⁢(n)=eγ

where γ is the Euler–Mascheroni constant.
