Silverman-Toeplitz theorem


Let {am⁢n} be a double sequence of complex numbers and let B be a positive real number such that:

  1. 1.

    ∑n=0∞|am⁢n|≤B for all m=0,1,2,…

  2. 2.

    limm→∞⁡∑n=0∞am⁢n=1

  3. 3.

    For every n=0,1,2,…, it is the case that limm→∞⁡am⁢n=0

Then, if the sequence {zn} converges, the series ∑n=0∞am⁢n⁢zn converges and

limn→∞⁡zn=limm→∞⁡∑n=0∞am⁢n⁢zn
