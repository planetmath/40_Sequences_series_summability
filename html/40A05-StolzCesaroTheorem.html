Stolz-Cesaro theorem


Let (an)n≥1 and (bn)n≥1 be two sequences of real numbers. If bn is positive, strictly increasing and unbounded and the following limit exists:

limn→∞⁡an+1-anbn+1-bn=l

Then the limit:

limn→∞⁡anbn

also exists and it is equal to l.

Remark. This theorem is also valid if an and bn are monotone, tending to 0.
